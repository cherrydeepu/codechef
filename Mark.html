Samantha Ruth Prabhu is a renowned Indian actress known for her work in Telugu and Tamil cinema. Born on April 28, 1987, in Chennai, she made her acting debut with*Ye Maaya Chesave* (2010), which brought her instant recognition. Over the years, she has delivered several blockbuster films like *Eega*, *Theri*, *Mahanati*, *Super Deluxe*, and *Oh! Baby*, showcasing her versatility across different roles. In addition to films, she gained widespread acclaim for her performance in the web series *The Family Man 2*, where she played the antagonist Raji. Apart from acting, Samantha is actively involved in philanthropy through her NGO, Pratyusha Support, which provides medical aid to women and children. Despite facing personal challenges, including her diagnosis with Myositis, she remains an inspiration to many with her resilience and positive outlook. Her dedication to her craft and strong presence in the industry have made her one of the most admired actresses in Indian cinema.
